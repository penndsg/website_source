About Us

We are a student organization at the University of Pennsylvania dedicated to helping students prepare for a career in data science.
Here are some of the things we do:

Datathons

We hold datathons to give students a fun opportunity to dive into real-world datasets and win cash and prizes.

Workshops

We hold workshops to demonstrate data science tools and techniques

Speakers

We bring in data professionals to talk to students about their fields and careers
