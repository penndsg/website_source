We are a student organization at the University of Pennsylvania dedicated to helping students prepare for a career in data science.

What We Do

Projects

We split students into small teams to work on practical, self-directed data science projects

Workshops

We hold regular workshops to demonstrate data science tools and techniques

Career Talks

We bring in data professionals to talk to students about their career paths

Want to Join?

Whether you want to work on a team project or you're just interested in a weekly mailing list of data science events, sign up here (free!) and we'll hook you up.
